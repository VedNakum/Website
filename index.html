Why Choose Us?

  • Our goal is to provide your loved one with a place as well as an environment to call their own. The cheerful atmosphere of our home will make anyone living in our home feel like this is a place to belong. Because we are an owner operated facility, you can expect to see us involved in every aspect of home life. More importantly, we are always present for the residents every need.

  • We give frail seniors a safe and loving HOME where they not only receive competent and loving personal care, but also - and most importantly - feel needed and cared for. Therefore, they can enjoy a meaningful existence to the maximum extent of their individual capacity. At Caring Professionals AFC Homes, every staff member participates and contributes to their residents' lives in meaningful ways. Sometimes, simple tasks such as folding laundry, making brownies, or picking herbs from our garden planters can be the most effective way to enrich an individual's life, no matter what his or her medical condition is. It's about encouraging residents to participate in their own lives, in offering an opportunity for meaningful contribution that rewards their sense of purpose and self.

  • Life at Caring Professionals AFC Home means amenities and services to enhance your lifestyle, and new opportunities to connect with others. We exist to make a difference in people's lives through excellent resident care.

  • Focus on care. If you are concerned about your loved one needing help and not being able to receive it, you wont have to. For every six residents, there are one to two caregivers depending on needs. This allows the caregiver to be more available to the residents and to be able to help in emergency situations more readily

  • Attention To Detail. Have you ever heard the saying The difference between something good and something great is attention to detail? Well, we take that very seriously at Caring Professionals AFC Home. The staff and residents truly get to know one another, and there is great attention paid to those who need it. For example, the caregiving staff routinely tracks food and water consumed by each resident per shift, and more. This attention to detail enables them to take better care of the residents and to also take action if they notice something to be amiss.

  • Trained, Caring & Dedicated Staff What would a personal care home be without great caregivers? Nothing! As a Caring Professionals, one of the principal qualities that is looked for during the hiring process is someone who has a caring heart. The owners value a passion for caring for seniors above all else. Each staff member is trained in house for each residents needs. Training takes place regularly throughout the year so that the staff stays up to date on recent and efficient caregiving skills and techniques

  • Intimate, Small Environment Focused On Care Caring Professionals AFC Home is a smaller personal care home. We are located in a quiet, residential area. The owners work hard to make each resident feel as though they are home. All rooms are ADA rooms that are designed to be open and functional. Seniors can enjoy our dining in their own living room and walk just a few steps to relax outside after a nice, home-cooked meal.
